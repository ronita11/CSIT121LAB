Delicious Milk Tea

  • Recipe by: Ronita Poudel

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1/2 tablespoon of tea leaves
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 1/2 tablespoon of cinnamon powder

Making Procedures:

  1. Heat the saucepan on the stove.
  2. Add milk and water, let it boil.
  3. Add tea leaves and sugar.
  4. Add cinnamon powder.
  5. Boil for 5-10 minutes on medium flame.
  6. Remove from heat and pour into a cup.

I love this recipe because it is delicious, and when I drink this, my mind becomes fresh and relaxed.
